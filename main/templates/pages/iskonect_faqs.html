Frequently Asked Questions

What is myPUPQC?

MyPUPQC is a campus-specific website catered to the PUPQC community more specifically between PUPQC administrators, faculty, and all iskolar ng bayan enrolled in the campus.

What services are available in myPUPQC Portal?

myPUPQC offers a variety of services tailored to the needs of the PUPQC community, including but not limited to academic resources, extension services tools, administrative services, and professional development opportunities.

Where can I find the systems in myPUPQC?

You can find the systems within myPUPQC by navigating to the Services section. There, you'll find a list of available systems that you can access according to your needs. Simply click on the system you wish to use, and you'll be directed to the respective platform.

Who can access myPUPQC information systems?

myPUPQC is accessible to everyone within the PUPQC community, including students, faculty, and campus administrators. Additionally, myPUPQC is also accessible to alumni, beneficiaries, and others who are part of or willing to be part of PUPQC.

Can I access specific systems within myPUPQC?

Yes, depending on your role within the university community, you may have access to specific systems within myPUPQC tailored to your needs.

What should I do if I need to contact the university regarding my inquiry?

If you have an inquiry and in need to contact the university using a hotline, please refer to the contact us section of the myPUPQC portal for further assistance.
